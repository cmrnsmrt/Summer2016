27th June 2016

Today was the day we left for Fellbach, we met outside the school door at 4am where we left on the bus to Edinburgh airport. When we arrived at the airport we all went for a coffee and some breakfast. Our flight was delayed by 20 minutes but we got there alright. To get to our final destination we had to take a train then bus. The train was a German S-Bahn (Street Train [Although mainly underground]). When we got on the train there were 33 of us but when we got off the train there were 32, we had left someone on the train. Someone had also lifted the missing persons luggage (phone too) as they went to tell everyone this was our stop. We were shortly reunited after Hannah got off at the next station and too a train back to us! We then took our bus to our partner school and met our host-families and spent the evening with them getting to know them.

28th June 2016

Today we went to school, we met at the "rondel" (a meeting point in the school hall) where we met our teachers and began our tour of the local town. We went to the bus stop near the school where we took the #60 bus to the center of Fellbach and started out Town tour and quiz, this consisted of us finding our way to a Cafe, being given a question sheet and told that we had an hour to complete it. First we visited the library to ask how many books were in the building. Then order lunch (a pretzel of course). We also had to go to the cinema and find out what film was showing that evening and answer more questions like that. Our team then got ice cream as we won the quiz competition. We then all went to the swimming pool that evening.





29th June 2016

Today we had lessons at the German school, We had engish lessons then a special cooking lesson where we learnt how to cook a local speciality which was a special dough based pasta and mountain cheese sauce. First we had to walk to the local supermarket to buy all of the ingredients and anything else we wanted. This took up most of the day then we went to the swimming pool again. Later in the evening there was a "get-together" at a local park. This was one of the more boring days compared to the other days.
